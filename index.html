Further information

Why do we ask for your e-mail?

Wargaming does not give us access to it.
Also due to phpbb security settings this is the only way to change it later on.
Thus if at any time you wish to change your e-mail, change it here, otherwise leave it blank.

Why do we need or want it?

We don't really. It's just for you in case you want to use forum notifications via e-mail.
So feel free to leave it completely blank.
